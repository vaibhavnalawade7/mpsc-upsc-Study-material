What is UPSC CSE Exam ?

The Civil Services Examination is a National-level competitive examination in India, which is conducted by the “Union public Service Commission” for recruitment to higher Civil Service officers of the government of India, Including IAS, IPs, IFS officers.

What are the Top jobs in UPSC exam ?

Indian Administrative Service (IAS)

India Police Services (IPS)

Indian foreign Service (IFS)

Thus, three are High demanding jobs in UPSC CSE , where most of the Aspirants first priority was IAS and IPS, after that IFS comes, and others.

Who is Eligible for UPSC CSE Exam ?

UPSC Civil Services Exam’s age limit is Candidate must be minimum of 21 year old and must not be more then 32 years old.

General category & EWS: 32 years (6 attempts), OBC (with certificate) 32 + 3 years (9 attempts), where SC/ST (32 + 5 years) unlimited attempts. can they give.

Qualification for UPSC CSE ?

Candidate can appear for other Government exams after 10+2 also. However, the list of Government exams and opportunities are more if the exams are taken after Graduation.

Qualification to give UPSC CSE is Candidate have to successfully cleared or pursuing last year of there Graduation with any streme, from any certified University of India only.

The Union public Service Commission conducts the IAS exam every year for the recruitment of officers to the Civil Service of India.

What is the salary of an IAS Officer ?

The Basic salary of an IAS officer starts at 56,100 rs. (starting level)-entry level, can go up-to 2,50,000 (maximum pay) -at cabinet secretary level.

Government also gives other facilities to IAS officers such as Home to live, Car to go office, servants to Home , etc (click here to know IAS officers allowance)
